Spells

Step into the realm of boundless possibilities with The Arch-Wizard's Handbook's enchanting Spells page. From the flick of a wand to the utterance of sacred words, unlock the secrets of spellcraft and awaken your inner wizardry. Whether you seek to command the elements, shape destinies, or manifest your deepest desires, our curated collection of spells offers a gateway to the extraordinary.

Fireball

Evocation

Required Materials: Powdered sulfur, bat guano, and alchemical oil.
Incantation: Mental focus and visualization are key elements in spellcasting. You must concentrate on the image of the fireball forming in your mind's eye and channel your magical energy into shaping and directing it towards your intended target.

Water Whip

Evocation

Required Materials: Blessed or purified water, and a harpy's feather.
Incantation: Focus your mind on the image of water coalescing and shaping into a whip-like form. Visualization is crucial for directing the flow of magical energy and shaping it into the desired manifestation.

Electro Storm

Evocation

Required Materials: A thunderstone, and distilled storm essence.
Incantation: Maintain unwavering focus on the image of a swirling vortex of electrical energy gathering around you, ready to be unleashed upon your enemies. Visualize the crackling arcs of lightning and the rumble of thunder, shaping the storm to your bidding.

Smoke Screen

Evocation

Required Materials: Powdered incense, a puff-shroom.
Incantation: Focus your mind on the image of smoke billowing and swirling, enveloping the area in a thick haze.
